04 — Straight answers

Questions, answered.

Are invoices from Lunga really compliant?

Every invoice includes all nine fields required for a valid tax invoice under Section 20 of the VAT Act — the words "Tax Invoice", supplier and recipient details with VAT numbers, serial number, date, description, quantity, value ex-VAT, VAT at 15%, and total inclusive. Lunga produces compliant documents, not tax advice. For a complex position, confirm with SARS or a registered tax practitioner.

I'm not VAT registered. Can I still use it?

Yes. Leave the VAT number blank and switch VAT off — Lunga produces a standard invoice without the VAT split. Ideal for sole traders under the R1 million threshold (rising to R2.3 million from 1 April 2026).

How does the free trial work?

Fourteen days, full product, no card — including payment links and branding. After that it becomes a paid plan. There is no permanent free tier.

Where is my data stored?

In your own browser. Nothing is sent to any server — Lunga has no backend. Your client list and figures never leave your device.

How do the payment links work?

Each invoice carries a payment link your client taps to pay by EFT, card, or PayPal. You set up a free payment account, add your merchant ID, and the link is generated per invoice.
